OUR STORY

Mumbai's Most Trusted Travel Partner

Founded in 2009 by Ganesh Kale, Prathamesh Tours & Travels began with a single vehicle and a simple promise — safe, reliable, and affordable travel for every Indian family. Over 15 years, we have grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted vehicle rental companies, completing over 10,000 trips across the length and breadth of India.

From snow-capped Himalayan passes to golden Goan beaches, from royal Rajasthan to serene Kerala backwaters — our drivers have navigated every terrain India has to offer. Our fleet ranges from compact sedans for solo travelers to 45-seater luxury coaches for large group yatras and corporate events.

At Prathamesh Tours & Travels, we believe every journey tells a story. Our mission is to make yours unforgettable — starting from the moment you book until you reach your destination, safely and comfortably.
